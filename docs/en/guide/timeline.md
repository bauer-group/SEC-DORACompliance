## BaFin-Specific Dates

BaFin offers annual workshops to prepare for the information register submission. The next submission period for 2026 is expected to take place in Q2 2026.

::: info Replacement of xAIT Circulars

- KAIT, VAIT, ZAIT: **Repealed since 16.01.2025**
- BAIT Chapter 11: **Repealed since 17.01.2025**
- BAIT complete: **Repeal on 31.12.2026**

Institutions falling under DORA have been exempt from BAIT since 17.01.2025.
:::
