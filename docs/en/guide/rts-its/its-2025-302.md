# ITS (EU) 2025/302 – Reporting Forms

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| **Number** | Implementing Regulation (EU) 2025/302 |
| **DORA Article** | Art. 20(1)(b) |
| **Pillar** | P2 – Incident Reporting |
| **Publication** | 20.02.2025 |
| **EUR-Lex** | [Link](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg_impl/2025/302) |

## Content

Standard forms, templates, and procedures for:

- Reporting major ICT-related incidents
- Notification of significant cyber threats

**Format:** XML (standardised, machine-readable)
**Authentication:** Qualified electronic certificates (eIDAS)
**Acknowledgement of receipt:** Automatic with unique incident number

→ XML template: [Incident Pipeline](/en/guide/automation/incident-pipeline)
